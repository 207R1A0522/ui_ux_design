User Interface(UI) -- What we see on screen
User Experience(UX) -- Which is experienced by the user
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Steps To Create User Interface(UI) :-
1)Sketch(roughly drawn manually)
2)Wireframe(geometrical shapes...better than sketch)
3)Mockup
4)Prototype(add new pages to the already creqated buttons)
5)Final Design(adds colours to the pages to clearly identify)
